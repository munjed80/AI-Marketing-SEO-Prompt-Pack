## üìö Mockup 3: 3D Book Standing on Dark Gradient Background

### Purpose
Create a premium, eye-catching hero image for sales pages and social media. Makes the digital product feel tangible and valuable.

### Detailed Description

**Book Details:**
- **Format:** Hardcover book standing upright
- **Dimensions:** Standard book proportions (6" x 9" appearance)
- **Thickness:** Medium thickness (~1.5-2 inches spine visible)
- **Cover:** The product cover design (use cover.svg or created cover)
- **Finish:** Glossy or semi-gloss (premium feel)
- **Position:** Standing upright, slight 3/4 angle view (both front cover and spine visible)

**Background:**
- **Type:** Dark gradient background (matches brand)
- **Gradient:** Dark navy (#0f172a) to deep purple (#1e293b)
- **Direction:** Top-left to bottom-right OR radial from center
- **Additional elements:**
  - Subtle light glow behind/around book (blue-purple hue)
  - Optional: Geometric shapes in background (circles, lines) at low opacity
  - Optional: Particles or dots floating around (tech feel)

**Lighting:**
- **Main light:** Soft spotlight from top-left
- **Rim light:** Blue or purple edge light on right side of book
- **Ambient:** Subtle glow illuminating the background
- **Shadows:** Soft shadow beneath book on ground plane

**Special Effects:**
- **Reflection:** Subtle reflection of book on ground surface (polished floor effect)
- **Glow:** Soft blue-purple glow emanating from book edges
- **Depth:** Use lighting and shadow to create dimension
- **Focus:** Book is sharp, background is slightly soft

**Ground Plane:**
- **Surface:** Dark, reflective surface (like polished granite or glass)
- **Reflection:** Partial mirror reflection of book (50% opacity)
- **Fade:** Reflection fades toward edges

**Composition:**
- **Book position:** Slightly off-center (rule of thirds)
- **Viewing angle:** Eye-level or slightly above
- **Space:** Negative space on one side for text overlay (if needed)

**Color Harmony:**
- Book cover: Blues, purples, whites (from actual design)
- Background: Deep navy, slate, purple tones
- Lighting: Blue and purple accent lights
- Overall: Cohesive, premium, tech-forward

**Mood:**
Premium, high-value, innovative, professional, cutting-edge

---

### Instructions for Canva:
1. Search "3D book mockup" in Canva templates
2. Choose dark background mockup or solid color
3. Upload cover design to apply to book face and spine
4. Adjust background to dark gradient (use gradient tool)
5. Add glow effect (from elements, search "glow" or "light")
6. Add subtle geometric shapes in background (low opacity)
7. Apply shadow beneath book
8. Export as high-res PNG

### Instructions for Photoshop:
1. Find 3D book mockup PSD (search "smart object book mockup")
2. Replace smart object with cover design
3. Create dark gradient background layer
4. Add new layer, use soft brush for glows (blue/purple)
5. Create ground reflection: duplicate book, flip vertical, reduce opacity, add mask
6. Add rim lighting with dodge tool or overlay layers
7. Add geometric elements (optional, low opacity)
8. Final color grading: boost blues/purples, deepen shadows
9. Export as high-res PNG or JPEG

## üìè Export Specifications

### For Gumroad/Sales Pages:
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1200 x 1500 px (product shots)
- **Format:** PNG with transparency OR high-quality JPEG
- **File size:** Under 5 MB for web optimization
- **Ratio:** 3:4 or 4:5 for product images

### For Social Media:
- **Instagram:** 1080 x 1080 (square) or 1080 x 1350 (portrait)
- **Facebook:** 1200 x 630 (OG image)
- **Twitter:** 1200 x 675
- **LinkedIn:** 1200 x 627
- **Pinterest:** 1000 x 1500

### For Website Headers:
- **Wide:** 1920 x 1080 (16:9)
- **Banner:** 2400 x 800
- **Hero section:** 1600 x 900

## üí° Pro Tips

### Creating Mockups that Sell:

1. **Show the product in use** - Not just floating in space
2. **Use lifestyle context** - Help buyers visualize ownership
3. **Keep it clean** - Don't overcrowd with props
4. **Lighting matters** - Soft, professional lighting always
5. **Consistency** - Use same color palette across all mockups
6. **High resolution** - Always export at 2x minimum for retina displays
7. **Test thumbnails** - Mockup should look good when small
8. **A/B test** - Try different styles to see what converts better

### Common Mistakes to Avoid:

‚ùå Low resolution or pixelated images  
‚ùå Too many props distracting from product  
‚ùå Unrealistic lighting or shadows  
‚ùå Cover design not readable  
‚ùå Inconsistent branding across mockups  
‚ùå Overly complex backgrounds  
‚ùå File sizes too large (slow loading)  
‚ùå Generic stock photos that don't showcase your product
